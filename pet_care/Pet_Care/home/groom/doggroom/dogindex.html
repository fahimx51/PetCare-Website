Our Services

Full Groom

A full groom includes a bath, blow-dry, brush, ear cleaning, nail clipping, and a haircut of your choice. This service is ideal for dogs that need regular grooming to maintain their coat and skin health.

Bath and Brush

A bath and brush includes a bath, blow-dry, and brush. This service is ideal for dogs that have short or low-maintenance coats, or for dogs that need a freshen up between full grooms.

Nail Trim

A nail trim includes clipping and filing the nails to a comfortable length. This service is important for dogs that have long or sharp nails, as they can cause pain, injury, or damage to the furniture.

Special Treatment

A special treatment includes a choice of a spa package, a de-shedding treatment, a teeth cleaning, or a flea and tick treatment. These services are designed to pamper your dog and address any specific needs or issues they may have.
